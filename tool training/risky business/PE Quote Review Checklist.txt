COMMON ITEMS

Upstream and downstream processes		
-Are we integrating into an existing system?		
-Refurb job? – High risk of print/PLC program mismatch		
-Additional buffer hours for CD and CP needed 		
Straight forward concept solution yes/no		
Any new technologies?		
Footprint constraints?		
Properly spec’d technology (example: robot size, reach, capacity)		
All assumptions have been added to quote (Examples: bowlfeeder, grease, vision, etc.)		
CMM certification required?		
		
CYCLE TIME		
Understand the difference between Cycle Time and TAKT Time		
-Which is required by customer for run-off? 		
-Sub 5s is a higher risk - Use extreme caution!		
-FYI - For high speed application, the concept needs to incorporate a 25% safety factor! E.g. If the customer target is 10s we should scope 7.5s 		
Do we understand the sequence well?		
-Detailed description of Operation sequences		
-Estimated cycle time per step		
How much is done manually vs automatically?
